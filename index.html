PUTTING
YOUR FEET
FIRST

Our Professionals have the ability to treat your foot and ankle condition with caring, expert advice to help you walk pain free. Our detailed tailored treatment plans aim to address the underlying cause of your foot and lower limb pain. We are a progressive clinic that covers all aspects of the podiatry profession, emphasising on frequent training to ensure we provide evidence based treatments for all our patients.

A Simple, Affordable
Approach to Foot Health Care

Rebates are available for our treatment for yourself and children through your private health insurance. The actual rebate will depend on your level of cover with your private health insurance, but we strive to keep the cost affordable for all members of the community because we understand the importance of putting your best foot forward. If you would like to estimate your out-of-pocket expenses for treatment, contact us for details on the relevant item numbers to check the actual rebates with your private health insurer. We also have HICAPS facilities available.

Get pain-free and improve your quality of life

Foot and ankle pain is not normal and it is important it is addressed at all stages of life.

Our team is comprised of a podiatry assistant, 2 caring Podiatrists and a visiting Specialist Podiatric Foot and Ankle Surgeon
allowing us to be uniquely positioned to help you, no matter what your foot, ankle and lower limb concerns may be.

Common conditions we help with include:

  • Arch and Heel Pain (Plantar Fasciitis)
  • Back of Heel Pain (Achilles Tendonitis)
  • Bunions
  • Children’s Foot problems (Flat Feet, In-toeing and more)
  • Ingrown Toenails
  • Plantar Warts
  • Bony Prominences
  • Neuromas
  • Hammer Toes
  • PACT fungal nail treatment
  • Onsite diagnostic ultrasound
  • Shockwave treatment
  • 3D Scanner Technology for custom Orthotics
  • Dry needle treatment
  • Bone growth stimulator

So whatever your podiatry problem, we can help you!
