Ryan James Podiatry was established in 2011 in the Lake Cathie Health Complex. Our team is comprised of a podiatry assistant, 3 caring Podiatrists and a visiting Specialist Podiatric Foot and Ankle Surgeon allowing us to be uniquely positioned to help you, no matter what your foot, ankle and lower limb concerns may be.

Podiatrists are
foot health
experts

Podiatrists are foot health experts who are university-trained to diagnose, prevent, treat and rehabilitate medical and surgical conditions of the foot, ankle and lower limb. If you answer yes to the following questions a Podiatrist may be able to help you:

  • Have painful feet?
  • Experience leg pain?
  • Experience sore hips?
  • Find walking or standing uncomfortable?
  • Experience leg or foot pain during sport?
  • Have painful knees?
  • Need advice on correct footwear?

    • Whatever your podiatry problem may be, we can help you!
